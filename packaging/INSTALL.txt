║   TUBE-RIPPER DELUXE 2000  —  install on a Mac             ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

Works on Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3/M4) AND Intel Macs. Everything is
bundled — no Python, yt-dlp, ffmpeg or other installs needed.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
INSTALL (about 30 seconds)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Keep both items together in this unzipped folder, then:

1.  DOUBLE-CLICK  "Open Me First.command"

    If macOS warns it's from an unidentified developer:
    RIGHT-CLICK it → Open → Open.  (You only do this once.)

This one step does everything: it copies TubeRipper to your
Applications folder, unblocks it (macOS flags downloaded apps —
this clears that so the app and its bundled Python can run), and
launches it. TubeRipper opens in your browser at http://localhost:1337

From then on, just open TubeRipper from Applications.

(Order matters: run "Open Me First.command" BEFORE deleting this
 folder. If the app is already in Applications, the command still
 finds and unblocks it there.)

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
USING IT
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
•  It opens in your browser at http://localhost:1337 — bookmark it.
   Paste a YouTube link, ANALYZE, pick a quality, RIP IT. Files go to
   your Downloads folder by default (change it in the SAVE TO box).

•  For HD / 4K: the SESSION COOKIES menu auto-picks your browser.
   Make sure you're signed in to YouTube in that browser.

•  Playlists & channels: paste the playlist/channel link to
   download many videos at once.

•  To stop it: click the  ⏻ QUIT APP  button on the page.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
NOTES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
•  100% local. Nothing is uploaded anywhere; it only fetches the
   video from YouTube on your own machine.
•  Phone / other devices: click "USE ON PHONE" — it shows a stable
   web address (like macbook-pro.local:1337) and a 6-digit PIN. On the
   same Wi-Fi, open that in your phone's browser, enter the PIN once,
   and bookmark it. (Away from home: install Tailscale, free, on both.)
•  Only download videos you own or are licensed to download.
